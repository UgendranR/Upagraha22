About us

Our College

Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering

Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (SVCE), a premier self-financing engineering college was started in the year 1985 and is managed by Sri Venkateswara Educational and Health Trust. The college attained autonomous status in 2016. SVCE has 12 under graduate and 7 post graduate programmes in Engineering and Technology. The college has 11 research centers recognized by the Anna University, Chennai, which cater to MS (by Research) and Ph.D programmes. The courses are approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The college is accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with A+ grade. The college is also certified by IRQS for ISO 9001:2015.

UPAGRAHA '22

The flagship technical symposium of the ECE department of Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering is back as a two-day fest with new and exciting events. Upagraha 22 will enhance the technical knowledge of students from various engineering colleges and provide them with a platform to exhibit their talents. It helps students to identify and understand the various aspects and provides them with opportunities to develop their versatility and charisma in the divergent aspects of their growth.

Upagraha '22

Upagraha '22 will act as a platform to exchange ideas and scientific knowledge through intense professional interaction.
